Overview of methods

Generative adversial networks (in short GANs) is class of neural network architectures composed to achieve the best state of art results in generating new data that maintains statistics of a training dataset. The model in basic version consists of two networks called generator and discriminator. They play zero-sum game where discriminator is trained to distinguish between real data from dataset and fake from generator and generator is trained to maximize loss of a discriminator.
